About XOXCO

XOXCO is a software product design and development studio. We create cutting-edge experiences through software. We believe technology should work for people. The company was founded in 2008 by Ben Brown and Katie Spence.

Brown’s career in tech began in the '90s. He's had roles ranging from server administrator to CEO. He is an Internet publishing pioneer, and has been the founder of several startups, most notably the tags-based dating site Consumating.com, which was acquired by CNET Networks in 2005. After a few years in Silicon Valley, Brown convinced Spence to leave her gig at a San Francisco design studio to start their own back home in Austin.

In the last six years, we have worked with startups to define, develop and launch products. We have worked on R&D projects with Fortune 500 companies. We have built a talented team of designers and developers who know how technology works, and who think creatively about software.

User experience. Product design. Software development. Prototypes, web sites, mobile apps, and APIs. It's all in our wheelhouse.

We’ve launched products. We’ve been through IPOs and acquisitions. We’ve helped dozens of organizations design, build and launch products - many of whom we work with still.

We make things because it is fun and we want to know how things work. We'd like to make something for you. Get in touch.
